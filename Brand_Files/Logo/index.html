05 — How to Upload

Steps for LinkedIn

  1. Open this page in Chrome, Edge, Safari or Firefox. Wait a moment for Space Grotesk and DM Sans to load (the wordmark uses Space Grotesk 300).
  2. Click any Download PNG button above. A PNG renders client-side from the SVG and downloads to your device — no server upload, no quality loss.
  3. For your company / profile picture on LinkedIn, upload ayuvinc-linkedin-profile-dark.png (400 × 400 or 800 × 800).
  4. For your profile cover banner, upload ayuvinc-linkedin-banner-dark.png (1128 × 191 or 2256 × 382).
  5. If LinkedIn rejects PNG for any reason, re-export as JPG via your OS preview tool. PNG with the navy ground is preferred — JPG works as a fallback only.
