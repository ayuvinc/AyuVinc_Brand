04 — Context & Constraints
AyuVinc is
— The master brand. Text only. No symbol.
— English only. Never carries Devanagari.
— Space Grotesk 300 in all contexts.
— The parent of Dhara (01), Vaani (02), Setu (03).
— Globally readable. Institutionally credible.
AyuVinc is never
— Combined with a symbol or abstract mark.
— Displayed with Devanagari.
— Rendered in weights above 400.
— Used in a colored or gradient fill.
— Centered on a layout (always left-aligned in lockups).
