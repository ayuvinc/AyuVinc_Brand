For Vidushi

Yoga Studios

💡 Go for Hatha or Yin yoga (gentle, restorative). Avoid fast-paced Ashtanga or hot yoga. Morning sessions (7–9 AM) are the sweet spot.
Parmarth Niketan
Hatha · Guided Meditation
Most well-known ashram — drop-in morning classes, right on the Ganges. Also hosts the Ganga Aarti.
📍 Directions
Vinyasa Yoga Academy
Vinyasa · Yin Yoga
Walking distance from Tapovan — easiest drop-in option. Great Yin batch available.
📍 Directions
Anand Prakash Ashram
Hatha · Ashtanga
Very popular with international visitors. Request the gentle Hatha morning batch specifically.
Sivananda Ashram
Classical Sivananda
Traditional, quiet, highly authentic. Great for a one-off immersive session.
Omkarananda Ganga Sadan
Gentle Yoga · Pranayama
Breathing-focused, easy pace — ideal for a truly restorative session.
Logistics

Getting Around

🛺
Auto-Rickshaw
Best for short hops between Tapovan, Ram Jhula, Laxman Jhula
Shared auto: ₹20–40 per person
Private: ₹150–300
Autos can't cross pedestrian bridges — drop off and walk over
🛵
Scooty Rental
Most flexible — rent for the day and go at your own pace
Where: Main Tapovan road or near Laxman Jhula
Cost: ₹400–600/day
Carry your DL — usually not checked but good to have
